This Privacy Policy describes how **Car Plate Detector** handles information when you use the app.

## Summary
- The app uses the **camera** to detect license plates in real time.
- Processing happens **locally on your device**.
- The app **does not upload** camera frames, photos, or videos to any server.
- The app **does not collect, store, or sell** personal data.

## Information the app processes
### Camera
The app accesses the camera to show a live preview and to perform on-device license plate detection. Camera frames are processed in memory on your device and are not transmitted off-device.

### Sound
The app may play a beep sound when a plate is detected. The app does **not** record audio.

## Data collection and sharing
- No user account is required.
- The app does not use advertising SDKs.
- The app does not collect analytics identifiers or location data.
- The app does not share data with third parties.

## Data retention
Because the app does not upload or store camera content or personal data, there is no server-side data retention.

## Changes to this policy
If the app changes in a way that affects privacy (for example, adding analytics, cloud processing, or OCR export), this policy will be updated and a new effective date will be published on the same page.
